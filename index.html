What settings should I use?

Most users will want the Use Metagroup option set to the all metagroup.
Ignore the Active Groups option unless you know what you're doing!

Pick the base metagroup if you do not intend to fix the level after conversion.
This option preserves level hitboxes, but may delete extra objects.

Pick the all metagroup if you intend to fix the level after conversion.
This option keeps the most visual compatibility, but allows hitbox changes.

You should always reverify your level after conversion.
Ignoring visual changes, the physics of 1.9 are slightly different than 2.1's.
The base metagroup is not an excuse to be lazy!
